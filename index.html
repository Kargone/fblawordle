FLBA Wordle

Hexa-Word

Rules
- You have seven tries to guess the randomly selected 6-letter word
- Depending on what the word is the letters will either be green, yellow, or stay the same color
- Press the "enter" key to submit a 6-letter word
Green
- Shows you that you have a letter in the correct spot
Yellow
- Shows you that the letter is in the wrong spot
Stays the same
- Shows you that the letter is not in the final word
Easy
- You will be given one letter
Normal
- Normal wordle just six letters
Random
- Random string of six letters
